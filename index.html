Who we are

Aftertaste is the social hub for Movie and TV fans who trust people over algorithms.

Track what you watch, compare tastes, and get real recommendations from friends, creators, and critics — all in one place.

Because finding your next favorite thing shouldn’t feel like scrolling forever.

A Menu of Features

  • Track & Rate

    Log watched and in-progress items, score from 1–10, and keep your history tidy.

  • Tastebuds

    Follow friends and creators; compare tastes and see what your circle is watching.

  • Lists

    Curate themes and share—like “Mindf*ck Movies”, your crowd-pleasing rabbit hole.

  • Reviews & Discussions

    Post reviews, react, and dive into threads that actually help you decide what to watch.
